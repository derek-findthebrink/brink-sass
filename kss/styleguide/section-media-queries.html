7.1.1@mixin layoutBP #permalink

  • $point
    point (unit of measurement, usually px) at which to break on specified feature, defaults to 768px
  • $type
    feature upon which to break viewport at, defaults to max-width
  • @content
    expressions to evaluate at breakpoint
